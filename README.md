pathfinder-sandbox
a simple grid thing to watch dfs, bfs, ucs, and a* find paths. you can drag the green and red squares around and click to draw walls or yellow traffic.

how to use:
pick an algorithm.

click the white boxes to draw walls or yellow traffic (yellow costs more).

drag the green (start) and red (end) squares where you want.

hit Go! and watch it work.

the logic:
DFS/BFS: the basic ones from the python project.

UCS: hates traffic, takes the long way to save "fuel."

A*: the smart one that uses math to find the goal faster.
